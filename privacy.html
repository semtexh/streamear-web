Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

StreamEar ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Location Preferences: You may select geographic regions of interest to receive relevant alerts
  • Notification Preferences: Settings for push notifications and alert types
  • App Settings: Language preferences, voice settings, and other customization options

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, alerts viewed, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics to improve app stability

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your precise GPS location
  • Personal identifiable information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information (free app)
  • Contacts or photos
  • Browsing history outside the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver real-time alerts relevant to your selected regions
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Send push notifications based on your preferences
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Detect and prevent security threats

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Alert history
  • Saved alerts
  • Read/unread status
  • App settings

3.2 Cloud Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Backend database for alert distribution (hosted in US data centers)
  • Cloudflare Workers: Real-time alert processing and delivery
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): Push notification delivery

3.3 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure API authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • No sale of user data to third parties

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

4.1 Content Sources

We aggregate publicly available information from:

  • News RSS feeds (Reuters, BBC, France24, DW News, etc.)
  • Social media APIs (X/Twitter via twitterapi.io)
  • Public emergency alert systems

4.2 AI Processing

  • Google Gemini API: Alert classification and translation (data not retained by Google)

5. Push Notifications

Push notifications are delivered via Apple's Push Notification Service (APNs). We only send notifications for:

  • Breaking news alerts matching your region preferences
  • Critical emergency notifications (when enabled)
  • Pattern detection insights (optional)

You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → StreamEar → Notifications.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Device Data: Retained until you delete the app or manually clear data
  • Server-Side Alerts: Stored for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all locally stored data within the app
  • Delete: Clear all app data via Settings → Data Management
  • Control: Manage notification preferences and region filters
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics in app settings

8. Children's Privacy

StreamEar is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

StreamEar is available globally. By using the app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States, where our servers are located. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for European users.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Updated "Last Modified" date at the top of this page

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
